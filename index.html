Rules Sheet

Attendance is an essential for all campus-based programmes of study,full-or part-time, if you are a full-time student you must reside in Leicester or within easy commuting distance of the city for the duration of each semester.All students are normally required to attend all learning and teaching sessions associated with the course on which they are resistered.

Absence: requests and notifications

  • It is responsiblity of every student to notify their home department of any absences from the univirsity.
  • If you are unable to attend a class,tell your department/school office or your personal tutor straightway
  • when the absence is a result of illness of less than five days' duration you should complete and submit a self-certification form.
  • Department are empowered by Senate Regulation 4 to authorise short absences for personal or religious responses from campus-based courses
  • Requests for absences of more than one week must be explicitily in writing and only will granted if the department is in agreement with the proposal and you take full responsiblity for the completion of outstanding academic work.

Non-authorised absence

  • Styling who are absent from compulsory teaching sessions without an accepted explanation risk disciplinary action.
  • Your department will contact you after a period of non-authorised attendance,to advise the next steps, after assistance and explain any action that may be taken.This will be conducted in accordance with the stages laid out in the university's policy on Attendance at Timetabled Teaching Events

    • If you are struggling to meet attendance requirements

    • Contact your personal tutor to discuss your situation if you are strugging to fulfil the attendance or any other requirements of your course.
    • If your personal tutor is not available you can speak with your Head of Department or an appropriate member of staff in the department/school office.
